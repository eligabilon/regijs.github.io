Considere a matriz \[A=\begin{bmatrix}2 & 1 & -1\\ 1 & 2 & 1\\ -1 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}\] Qual das alternativas representa uma base ortonormal para o subespaço definido por $(A-3I_3)X=\bar{0}$?
$\left\{(-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}},0,\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}), (\frac{1}{\sqrt{6}},\frac{\sqrt{6}}{3},\frac{1}{\sqrt{6}})\right\}$.
$\left\{(\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}},-\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}},\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}})\right\}$.
$\left\{(-1,0,1),(\frac{1}{2},1,\frac{1}{2})\right\}$.
$\left\{(1,-1,1)\right\}.$
